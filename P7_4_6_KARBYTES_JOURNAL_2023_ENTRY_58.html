I apparently have been offered what looks like a full-time job doing package handling for a company named OnTrac in Fremont, California (night shifts Monday through Friday). Apparently, the background check was completed within 48 hours with no problematic results. The only “issue” which came up was that my (original copy) birth certificate was too tattered to be considered acceptable as as an identity verification document. I was told to go to a local Social Security office to order a social security card (which I did) and then to show the receipt to a manager. (Last night I got one drawing done, but it I see and remember it being hastily colored. Perhaps before my first work shift tonight I will try to make a better drawing to make up for how (relatively) shitty I think that previous drawing is. I am worried that I am becoming dangerously sleep deprived, but after tonight’s shift I will have two days off to catch up on rest. Also, I expect to get paid every Friday via direct deposit to my Patelco (credit union) checking account at a rate of $18.50 per hour for approximately 40 hours per week (which is approximately $600 weekly (rounded down to compensate for the amount of my wages which are collected as taxes and spent on transfer fees and small miscellaneous expenses))). One last thing: I published all the drawings which I published (and still have not removed) on Instagram, Twitter, and Minds within the past two months to my Patreon page. Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com has been taking a much deserved vacation from constantly being updated and fussed over. (To be more technical, my primary website (Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com) is currently in “production mode” and not in “development mode” (but I will eventually switch that website back to “development mode” when I feel that it is time to make updates to that website (i.e. add new pages to that website))). I may or may not add new content to that website before I receive my first paycheck from what appears to be my new job.
